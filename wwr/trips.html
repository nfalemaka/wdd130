White Water Rafting Trips

The Services We Offer!

Keep it calm

Keep it calm is our layback trips where we focus more on rafting on the more calming waters and enjoy what nature has to offer. If you want very easy going and not to get too wet, this is the perfect trip for you!.

Adventure Awaits

If you are looking to get the most excitement in water rafting, Adventure Awaits is for you! Our tour guides will take you to where the water is most chaotic for a thrilling ride. Safety rules will be advised. This ride will also get you the most wet but the most fun from the water.

Three Full Days

We also offer trips for three days where our tour guides will not also get you where the water is most thrilling but also where it's calm. This one is a mix of Adventure Awaits and Keep it Calm but for three whole days. Details of time are discussed individually. Please note that this is not a partial trip. It has to be three days in order for the dsicounted charge to be applied.

Want to be by yourself?

Other services we offer is our single kayaking and paddleboards lessons to anyone who is interested. We set up trips for individuals who want to go and be on their own with no tour guide. We also offer trips with a tour guide if you prefer having company. This is a fun way to get out and enjoy nature without dealing with the chaos of water rafting.
