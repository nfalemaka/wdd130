White Water Rafting

Our purpose is to provide a safe and fun environment for families and individuals who enjoy water rafting while also providing helpful tips so you can share with friends and build happy, long lasting memories! We service families and individuals, men, women and children of ages that are old enough to enjoy water rafting and all our company has to offer! Come join us for a safe and fun-filled water rafting experience! Our employess are all well trained and experts to ensure a well water rafting trip! We never serve our customers without a genuine smile on our faces.

History

The company was established ten years ago when a father decided to create a small water rafting company for his children to enjoy. Today, that company has expanded and is now located in three locations in the valley to ensure everyone's safety and enjoyment while water rafting. This is a locally family owned business whose founder, Mr. George Louis, has now passed on his business to our current owner Mr. Henry Wallace, his grandson. Mr. Wallace believes in sharing everything his grandfather believed in when he started the company which is to help families and individuals have lasting, happy memories with those around them that not only will help them build stronger and better bonds but lasting memories for a lifetime.
